Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 11, 2026

Spec ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how our mobile application handles information when you use it.

1. Information We Collect

Offline Local Usage: By default, the application runs entirely on your device using local storage. We do not collect, transmit, or store any personal information on private servers during offline usage. Your drink history, ratings, and flavor preferences remain stored locally on your physical device.

Menu Scanning (Camera / Photo Library): The app requires Camera and Photo Library permissions to let you photograph or upload drink menus. Images are used strictly to extract drink text locally and are processed temporarily. We do not store or upload your menu images permanently to any database.

Cloud Profile Sync (Optional): If you choose to authenticate and create an account, your flavor profile, favorites, and menu history are synced securely using Firebase Services (Firestore database) solely to enable sync across your devices.

2. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services to process features inside the app:

  • Google Gemini API / Generative Language API: Used to analyze menu text lists and generate cocktail recommendations. Text extracted from menus is sent temporarily to Google Gemini endpoints. This data is not linked to your personal identity.
  • Google Firebase (Firestore & Auth): Used to handle authentication and database sync if you choose to sign up.

3. Children's Privacy

Our application references cocktails and is intended strictly for users of legal drinking age. We do not knowingly collect personal data from anyone under the age of 18 or 21 (depending on local jurisdiction).

4. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, you can reach out to us at: support@specapp.com.
