Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 11, 2026

Spec ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how our mobile application handles information when you use it.

1. Information We Collect

On-Device Storage Only: The application does not require an account and runs entirely on your device. Your drink history, ratings, flavor preferences, favorites, and scan history are stored only in your device's local storage. We do not operate user accounts and do not store this information on our servers. You can permanently erase all of it at any time using the "Reset Data" option in the app.

Menu Scanning (Camera / Photo Library): The app uses Camera and Photo Library permissions to let you photograph or upload a drink menu. To read the menu, the menu image or pasted menu text is sent to the Google Gemini API for analysis (see Section 2). We do not retain these images or text on our own servers after the menu is processed.

2. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party service to process menus inside the app:

  • Google Gemini API / Generative Language API: Used to read menu images or text and identify drinks so we can generate a recommendation. The menu image or text is sent to Google's endpoints for processing. This request is not linked to a personal account or identity. Google's handling of this data is governed by Google's own privacy policy and API terms.

3. Children's Privacy

Our application references cocktails and is intended strictly for users of legal drinking age. We do not knowingly collect personal data from anyone under the legal drinking age in their jurisdiction.
